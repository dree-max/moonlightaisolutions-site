About Us

We're on a mission to help entrepreneurs successfully integrate AI into their businesses. Founded in Kampala, Uganda, we're bringing world-class AI automation to businesses across Africa and beyond.

Our Story

MoonlightAI Solutions was founded in 2025 with a simple belief: every entrepreneur deserves access to powerful AI tools, regardless of their technical background or location.

We saw that many businesses were either not using AI at all, or implementing it in ways that their teams wouldn't actually adopt. The typical AI implementation sees only 15-30% team adoption. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our process-first approach focuses on understanding your workflows before implementing any technology. This ensures that when we deploy AI solutions, your team is ready to embrace them‚Äîresulting in 80%+ adoption rates within 8-12 weeks.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, we're proud to be part of the growing African tech ecosystem. We believe Africa is poised to leapfrog traditional technology adoption and become a leader in AI implementation.
