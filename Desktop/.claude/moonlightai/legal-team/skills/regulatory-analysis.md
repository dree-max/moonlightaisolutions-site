# Regulatory Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Analyze regulatory frameworks, compliance requirements, licensing issues, and multi-jurisdictional regulatory landscapes for businesses.

## When to Use
- Analyzing regulatory requirements for new products/services
- Compliance audits
- Licensing applications
- Multi-state regulatory analysis
- Industry-specific regulatory mapping
- Government enforcement defense

## Regulatory Frameworks Covered

### Financial Services
- Securities regulations (SEC, state blue sky)
- Banking regulations (OCC, FDIC, Federal Reserve)
- Money transmission licensing
- Investment advisor requirements
- Consumer financial protection

### Technology & Data
- Data privacy (CCPA, GDPR, state laws)
- Data breach notification
- COPPA, BIPA, state privacy laws
- AI regulation and emerging frameworks
- Cybersecurity requirements

### Healthcare
- HIPAA compliance
- State healthcare licensing
- FDA regulations (if applicable)
- Telehealth regulations

### Employment
- FLSA and wage/hour
- State employment laws
- Independent contractor classification
- Background check regulations

### Industry-Specific
- Telecommunications
- Transportation/rideshare
- Food and beverage
- Real estate
- Education

### Environmental
- EPA regulations
- State environmental laws
- Carbon/ESG reporting

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Regulatory Mapping
For a given business/activity:
- Identify all applicable regulatory bodies
- Map federal, state, and local requirements
- Note jurisdictional variations
- Identify licensing/permit requirements

### 2. Compliance Assessment
For each requirement:
- What's required?
- Deadline/timeline?
- What's the penalty for non-compliance?
- What's the cost of compliance?
- Is compliance achievable?

### 3. Risk Analysis
- Enforcement risk (likelihood and source)
- Financial exposure (fines, penalties, liability)
- Reputational risk
- Business interruption risk

### 4. Practical Recommendations
- Prioritized compliance roadmap
- Quick wins vs. long-term projects
- External counsel needed?
- Regulatory engagement strategy

## Multi-Jurisdiction Analysis

### Federal vs. State
- Preemption analysis
- State-by-state variations
- Home vs. target state requirements

### US vs. International
- Cross-border considerations
- GDPR, international privacy laws
- Foreign corrupt practices
- International sanctions

### Comparison Tables
Use tables to present multi-jurisdiction analysis:
```
| Requirement | Federal | California | New York | Texas |
|-------------|---------|-------------|----------|-------|
| License     | Yes     | Yes         | Yes      | No    |
| Renewal     | Annual  | Biennial    | Annual   | N/A   |
| Fee         | $X      | $Y          | $Z       | N/A   |
```

## Quality Standards
- Primary source verification (cite actual regulations)
- Currency check (is the regulation still in effect?)
- Exception awareness (exemptions, safe harbors)
- Recent enforcement trends
- Flag proposed/changing regulations
